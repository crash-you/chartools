Phase 1: Ideation & Draft Creation

1. Define Goals & Framework

Before starting to write, clarify these elements:

  • Target Audience: Identify reader demographics and reading habits
  • Content Goals: Information sharing, emotional connection, or call-to-action
  • Length Constraints: Platform requirements or media specifications
  • Publishing Channels: Different platforms have different formatting requirements
