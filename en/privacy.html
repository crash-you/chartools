Privacy Policy

Updated: 2025-08-30

We value your privacy. This website is a pure client-side application where all text processing occurs locally in your browser. We do not collect, store, or upload any text content you enter into our tools.

Information We Collect

Text Processing Data

  • Local Processing: All text content you enter in our tools is processed locally in your browser and is never uploaded to our servers.
  • Data Security: We cannot access, store, or view any text content you input.

Analytics Data

  • We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous website usage statistics, including page views, user geographic location (country/region level), device type, etc.
  • This data is used to improve website performance and user experience and does not include any personally identifiable information.
  • We have enabled Google Analytics IP anonymization feature to ensure your IP address is anonymized.

Advertising-Related Data

  • This website uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies and other technologies to provide relevant ads.
  • Google displays personalized ads based on your interests and browsing history. You can manage ad preferences through Google Ads Settings.

Cookie Usage

Essential Cookies

  • Language Preference: Remember your selected language setting
  • Cookie Consent Status: Record your consent status for cookie usage

Analytics Cookies

  • Google Analytics: Used for website traffic statistics and performance analysis
  • Data Retention: Analytics data is retained for 26 months

Advertising Cookies

  • Google AdSense: Used to display relevant ads and prevent ad fraud
  • Personalized Ads: You can opt out of personalized advertising services

Cookie Management

You can manage cookies through the following methods:

  • Disable or delete cookies in your browser settings
  • Browse in private/incognito mode
  • Manage advertising cookies through Google Ads Settings page

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

Google Analytics

  • Purpose: Website traffic statistics and user behavior analysis
  • Data Type: Anonymized access data, device information, geographic location (country level)
  • Privacy Policy: Google Privacy Policy

Google AdSense

  • Privacy Policy: How Google uses data on partner sites
